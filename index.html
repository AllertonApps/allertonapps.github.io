Boulder King puts you above a clean grid where every swipe sends you hurtling until you collide or fall off-screen. Plot the perfect sequence to shatter every stationary rock while mastering dashes, portals, and lock-and-key boulders across 40 handcrafted stages.

  • Momentum-only swipes demand careful ordering to avoid overshooting the playfield.
  • Dash tiles redirect your run mid-flight so you can thread impossible angles.
  • Portals pause the chaos and let you choose a fresh heading before the next collision.
  • Keys unlock reinforced boulders, adding long-form goals across the 40-level launch set.
