FitScribe Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-21

Review the FitScribe Terms of Service

This Privacy Policy describes how Allerton Apps, LLC (“Allerton Apps,” “we,” or “us”) handles information in the FitScribe mobile application (the “App”). We collect only what we need to provide a reliable training experience.

Overview

FitScribe keeps your information minimal and purposeful. We never sell your data. We use it only to deliver personalized workouts, maintain your subscription, and troubleshoot issues.

Data We Collect

We collect account details from Apple (for example, a device identifier and purchase receipts), the workout logs you enter, and whether you have an active subscription. Optional diagnostics may include crash reports or basic performance metrics.

How We Use Data

Your data powers workout generation, syncs your entitlements, and helps us understand how features perform. We also use it to respond to support requests you send us.

Processing & Storage

Allerton Apps processes data with reputable cloud providers that follow industry-standard security practices. Data may be stored in the United States or other regions where our infrastructure resides.

Sharing

We share data only with vendors who help us operate FitScribe, such as infrastructure providers, analytics tools, or Apple for subscription validation. These vendors must handle the data solely for the tasks we assign.

Analytics & Ads

If we enable analytics or advertising features, we honor platform-level consent controls. You can opt out at the system level where supported, and we will respect those choices.

Your Choices

You can delete workout logs in the App at any time. Uninstalling the App removes local data from your device. Contact us at support@allertonapps.com to request deletion of server-stored information or to ask about managing your subscription.

Retention

We retain data for as long as you maintain an account and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

Children

FitScribe is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data to us, please contact us so we can remove it.

International Transfers

If you access FitScribe outside the United States, your data may be transferred to the U.S. or other countries where we or our providers operate. We protect transfers using appropriate safeguards.

Security

We apply administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the data we manage. Despite our efforts, no system is completely secure, so use the App with that understanding.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect new features or legal requirements. We will post the revised policy in the App or on our website with the updated date.

Contact

Reach us at support@allertonapps.com with questions or requests regarding this policy.
