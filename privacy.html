Allerton Apps, LLC (“we”, “our”, “us”) builds mobile applications including FitScribe. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • App data you provide (e.g., workout logs) stored on your device and/or our servers if you opt in to cloud services.
  • Diagnostics (crash reports, basic analytics) to improve reliability; these may be provided by Apple or our service providers.
  • Account/subscription data (email, subscription status) when you create an account or make purchases.

How We Use Information

  • To provide app functionality and optional cloud features you enable.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve the app.
  • To manage purchases and subscriptions (App Store).

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with service providers (e.g., analytics, crash reporting, payment processing) under agreements that restrict their use.

Your Choices

  • You can request deletion of account data by emailing support@allertonapps.com.
  • You can opt out of optional analytics in app settings (if available).
