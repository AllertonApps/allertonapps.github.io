What To Include

The more detail you provide, the faster we can help.

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • The FitScribe app version (Settings → About)
  • A short description of what happened and the steps that led to it
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if available

Account & Data Requests

For privacy or account deletion requests, email support@allertonapps.com with the subject “FitScribe Data Request.” We verify ownership via your Apple ID receipt and complete requests within 7 days.

If you’re experiencing a health emergency, contact local medical professionals immediately. FitScribe is not intended to provide medical advice.
